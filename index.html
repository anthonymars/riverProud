River Proud

Charleston's 'River Proud Program' is an initiative of the Charleston Storm-water Department to recognize the citizens of Charleston who take action to reduce stormwater runoff and pollution by successfully managing the water on their property.

Charleston's River Proud Program

Charleston, WV is nestled in the convergence of two beautiful rivers, the Elk and the Kanawha. These rivers provide the city with a precious natural resource that many parts of the United States and the World are devoid of.
